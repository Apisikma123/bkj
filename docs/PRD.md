# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name**: BKJ Group Indonesia Corporate Website
**Domain**: bkjgroup.com
**Target Audience**: B2B Clients, Logistics Partners, Investors, Job Seekers
**Languages**: Indonesian, English

## 2. Objective
To build a world-class, premium corporate experience that establishes BKJ Group Indonesia as a highly trusted, scalable, and modern logistics and transportation enterprise.

## 3. Core Features
- **Multilingual Support**: Switch seamlessly between English and Indonesian.
- **Premium Motion UI**: Smooth, GSAP-powered animations, parallax effects, and page transitions.
- **Dynamic Content**: Full CMS for Blog, Services, Gallery, and Client Marquees.
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Floating, pulsating CTA for immediate business inquiries.
- **SEO & Accessibility**: Fully compliant with WCAG AA, schema.org integration, and dynamic meta tagging.

## 4. Subsidiaries Pages
Dedicated landing pages for:
1. PT Bintang Kepri Jaya
2. PT Batam Kepri Jaya
3. Koperasi TKBM Bintang Kepri Jaya

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- **Performance**: Lighthouse 98+ scores across all metrics.
- **Responsiveness**: Fluid scaling from ultra-wide down to mobile fold.
- **Security**: CSRF, XSS, and SQL Injection prevention out of the box with Laravel.
